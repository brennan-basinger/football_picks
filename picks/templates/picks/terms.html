SEC Football Pick'em Contest — Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: {{ effective_date|default:"May 7, 2025" }}

1. Contest Overview

This is a private, invitation-only contest where participants predict the winner of each Southeastern Conference (SEC) NCAA football game during the regular season. The Contest is conducted for entertainment purposes only and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the NCAA or SEC.

2. Eligibility

  • Must be 18 years or older to participate.
  • Must be directly invited by the Contest Organizer or approved after being referred by an existing participant.
  • The Organizer reserves the right to approve or deny any entry at their sole discretion.
  • Contest is private and not open to the general public.

3. Entry Fee & Prize Distribution

  • A $20 entry fee is required to participate.
  • All entry fees must be submitted before the first game of the season.
  • The entire prize pool is distributed as follows:
    • 1st Place: 50% of the pot
    • 2nd Place: 30%
    • 3rd Place: 20%
  • No portion of the pot is retained by the Organizer.

4. Submission of Picks

  • Picks must be submitted before the official contest start date — August 30, 2025.
  • Picks are locked after kickoff and cannot be changed.
  • Missed picks receive 0 points.
  • All picks must be submitted through the official website interface.

5. Scoring and Tie-Breakers

  • Participants receive 1 point for each correct pick.
  • Standings are based on the total number of correct picks across the season.
  • Tie-breakers are applied in this order:
    1. Total correct picks in the final week
    2. Even split of prize in case of persistent tie
