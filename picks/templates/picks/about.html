About the SEC Football Contest

Predict every SEC game and compete for cash prizes!

How It Works

Every contest entrant registers, then makes a pick (Win or Loss) for each Southeastern Conference game of the 2025 season. Your bracket locks automatically when the first kickoff happens.

Key Rules
  • $20 buy-in per entrant
  • 1st place wins 50% of the pot
  • 2nd place wins 30% of the pot
  • 3rd place wins 20% of the pot
Bracket Changes

Entrants may revise their picks up until the start of the first SEC game.

Once kickoff happens, all brackets lock and picks become visible to everyone.

Important Dates

  • Registration & Pick Submission Opens Now
  • Pick Submission Closes Sat, Aug 30, 2025
  • Contest End Nov 29, 2025
